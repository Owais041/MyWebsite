Indian Cafe Specials

Samosa

A crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat. Served with chutney for dipping.

Tandoori Chicken

A marinated chicken dish cooked in a clay oven, giving it a smoky flavor. Served with naan bread and raita.

Biryani

A fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, meat, and vegetables. Served with raita and sometimes a side of curry.
